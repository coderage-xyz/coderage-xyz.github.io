Once we became more familiar with the type of modes Facepunch was building s&box to accommodate, we moved our attention to building an FTL-inspired multiplayer experience where players work together to maintain and upgrade a spaceship. Each system you visit has any number of random encounters from friendly traders, distress beacons or even pirates that want your ship and won't be taking "no" for an answer.
